Who We Are

Let's Educate Kakuma in short LEK is a transformative initiative dedicated to bridging the information gap on scholarship opportunities for refugees and host communities in and around Kakuma Refugee Camp. Inspired by personal experiences of growing up at Kakuma, LEK was born out of the pressing need to provide timely and accurate access to educational opportunities.

Our Mission

To empower individuals at Kakuma Refugee Camp by connecting them to global and local scholarship opportunities, fostering hope, self-reliance, and pathways to personal and professional development.

Our Vision

To build a foundation for personal growth, self-reliance, and long-term development in Kakuma Refugee Camp, where every individual has access to opportunities that enable them to thrive.

What We Do

LEK operates as a digital hub where scholarship opportunities are curated, categorized, and made easily accessible. Here's how we help

Centralized Platform

A one-stop solution for scholarship opportunities tailored to the needs of refugees and host communities.

User-Friendly Interface

A simple and intuitive platform designed to cater even to those with limited digital literacy.

Smart Categorization

Scholarships are categorized by eligibility criteria such as academic qualifications, field of study, and demographics.

Direct Access

Users can browse opportunities and apply directly through provided links.

Why It Matters

Many young people at Kakuma miss out on life-changing opportunities due to a lack of access to timely and accurate information. LEK aims to change that by

Empowering Communities

Providing resources to overcome financial barriers and unlock potential.

Promoting Inclusivity

Ensuring that opportunities are accessible to all, regardless of digital literacy levels.

Fostering Growth

Creating pathways to post-secondary education, vocational training, and professional certifications.
