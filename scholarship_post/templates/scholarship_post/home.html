Who We Are

Camp2Campus is a scholarship opportunity hub committed to bridging the information gap for refugees in Kakuma Refugee Camp.

Rooted in lived experiences of growing up in Kakuma, Camp2Campus was founded out of a genuine need to ensure timely, accurate, and accessible educational opportunities for all.

Our Mission

To increase access to university education for refugees in Kakuma by looking for scholarship opportunities and providing technical application support.

Our Vision

To build a foundation for personal growth, self-reliance, and long-term development in Kakuma, where every individual has access to opportunities that enable them to thrive.

What We Do

Camp2Campus operates as a digital hub where scholarship opportunities are curated, categorized, and made easily accessible. Here's how we help:

Scholarship Hub

Central database with a filterable list of available university scholarship opportunities.

Mentorship Program

We connect applicants with past scholarship awardees to provide valuable guidance.

Application Support

We assist applicants with technical application support and guidance.

Notification Alerts

Applicants receive timely alerts about scholarship deadlines and updates.

Why It Matters

Higher education enables refugee youth to develop skills, earn qualifications, and become change-makers. It equips them to lead in identifying solutions to challenges affecting their communities.

We aim to address:

SDG 4

Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all.

Goal 4.3

Ensure equal access to affordable, technical, and higher education.

Goal 4.9

Expand higher education scholarships for developing countries, especially the least developed.
